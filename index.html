      About LensCraft

      Our Story

      Founded in 2015, LensCraft began as a passion project between three photographers who wanted to share their knowledge and experiences with the world. What started as a simple blog has grown into a comprehensive resource for photographers of all skill levels.

      Our mission is to inspire creativity, provide valuable educational content, and build a community where photographers can learn, share, and grow together.

      Meet Our Team

      Our Photography Philosophy

      We believe that photography is more than just capturing images—it's about telling stories, preserving memories, and seeing the world through a unique perspective. Every photograph has a story behind it, from the location where it was taken to the technical decisions made by the photographer.

      Our articles focus on three key aspects: the technical (camera settings, equipment), the artistic (composition, lighting), and the experiential (location, story, emotion). We provide detailed location information because we believe context matters—understanding where and why a photo was taken adds depth to the image.
